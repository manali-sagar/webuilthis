Why This Exists

Why this table exists.

  • Not a product-based founder, so can't go to the really cool hype breakfasts and dinners in the startup scene.

  • Not really raising money, so can't get in the same room and conversations with VCs for partnerships.

  • Not taken seriously as an actual founder — and still struggling with biz dev, getting customers, capacity planning, and pitching yourself.

The Formats

Different tables. Same room.

Each format is built for a different part of a service founder's year — partnerships, press, peer learning, and the ones where you just need to be around your people.

✦ No. 01

Roundtable Dinners.

Twelve service founders around one long table. Find community and seed partnerships and referrals.

✦ No. 02

VC × Operator 1:1 Match.

Speed-round intros with investors and operators who actually work with service businesses. Warm, short, and built for real partnerships.

✦ No. 03

Media Match Day.

Curated intros to writers, podcasters, and newsletter operators covering the service economy. Pitch-free format.

✦ No. 04

Fireside Sessions.

Honest conversations with service founders who've scaled past solo — what broke, what they'd do again, what they'd never.

✦ No. 05

Tactical Workshops.

Capacity planning, positioning, pricing, pitching yourself. The operational things nobody teaches service founders.
